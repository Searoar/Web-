What we do

We’re the government’s adviser for the natural environment in England. We help to protect and restore our natural world.

Natural England is an executive non-departmental public body, sponsored by the Department for Environment, Food & Rural Affairs.

Suspected poisoning of wildlife or companion animals by professional pesticide use

Telephone (Wildlife Incident Investigation Scheme)

0800 321 600

Contact the Wildlife Incident Investigation Scheme (WIIS) if you suspect wildlife or companion animals have been poisoned by professional pesticide use.

Do not report domestic incidents of poisoning to WIIS. Report domestic incidents to the police, RSPCA or your local council.

Seek immediate veterinary treatment if an animal or bird is suffering.

The WIIS voicemail is monitored from Monday to Friday, 9am to 4:30pm. It is not monitored during the weekend or public holidays.
